Toyota Lexus

Lexus is the luxury vehicle division of the Japanese automaker Toyota. The Lexus brand is marketed in more than 90 countries and territories worldwide and is Japan's largest-selling make of premium cars.

While, there is a tough competition in the market for luxury cars, Lexus remains on the top spot for having best-selling SUVs in it's fleet. Most of the Lexus cars, share their platforms and parts with Toyota cars.
